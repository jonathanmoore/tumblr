Getting Started

For quite some time I have planned on creating a blog to share my thoughts on the interactive industry, creativity, and technology.  Rather than continuing to put off launching a blog I have decided to go with the tumblr platform because of it’s ease of use and more informal nature.  Eventually I plan on customizing this blog and truly making it my home, but for now the most important thing is simply getting started.

I imagine this site will evolve over time, but the core objective of the site is three-fold:

  1. Share ideas liberally
  2. Provide a broad source of inspiration
  3. Fuel creative innovation

Please feel free to send me comments, questions, ideas to jmoore@newezra.com
