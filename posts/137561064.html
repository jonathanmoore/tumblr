Walk From Mexico to Canada

Cookie and Paul walked the entire 3,100 mile Continental Divide Trail that crosses through 5 states, 25 National Forests, 4 National Parks and 3 Indian Reservations.  Throughout the entire trek they blogged and documented their journey and survival through the wilderness.  The recently released documentary is full of beautiful scenery and great design.
