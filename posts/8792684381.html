Style Hatch Updates

Now that Style Hatch’s first employee Jess Brown has finally moved to southern California with his family, we’re charging full steam ahead on the future of Style Hatch and two other top-secret startups.  I’m loving the new energy in the office and looking forward to bringing on the next talented person—email me if you’re an advanced front-end developer interested in joining the team.

Charity Water


From the beginning of Style Hatch I have wanted to make giving a core part of the company’s culture.  In June we setup a Charity Water campaign with a goal of giving $15,000 to provide life changing clean water for 750 people.  Already with $7,200 we’re nearly half of the way there, and we should hit our goal in the next 37 days of the campaign.  Feel free to contribute. 

Theme Updates


Over the last few weeks we’ve pushed through massive updates to all of our existing themes to add in our new photoset plugin (yay for high resolution photosets!), support for the new Google +1 button, new social icons, major performance increases and bug fixes.  In all Atlantic Noir, Ascender, Backburner, Inspire Well, MARS, Nova, Savory,  Swell, and Transcender have thousands of new lines of code and improvements thanks to all the great feedback we have received.

Customer Experience a.k.a Support


In my opinion, quality support is far more important than the design and quality of the themes—although important.  When someone emails us it is many times our one shot at giving someone a personal experience with the Style Hatch brand.  Making customer experience and support a top priority has been our secret sauce in converting people into fans.  Plus, all emails that come in through support@stylehatch.co get an answer within a day or sooner.  As of today we have resolved 2,741 email requests!

New Themes

Right now we have four new themes in the works that we’re excited to launch.  Overall we’ll continue to focus on super high-quality, unique and highly customizable themes over launching as many themes as possible.  We’ll keep on pouring love into each of our themes!
