Adhemas Batista - I’m Selling Colors

Brazilian illustrator Adhemas Batista updated his personal portfolio to showcase new work for Windows 7, Coca Cola, Marlboro, Honda and others.  Adhemas is most widely know for his work for the Havaianas brand.  Currently he is located in Los Angeles running his own design studio Magenta.
