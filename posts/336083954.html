Penny Shooter Business Card

Cardnetics, maker of the internet-famous Cardapult, recently reviled their latest prototype that fires pennies across the room.  This brings back memories of school when would figure out how to fashion any type of shooting device out of rubber bands, mechanical pencils, and paperclips.  They’re selling prototype cards for $8 a piece.
