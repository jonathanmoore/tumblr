Seth Godin: Quieting the Lizard Brain

The more and more we create ideas and not follow through on shipping the ideas the harder it will become to make ideas happen.  The reverse is also true, as you make ideas happen to becomes easier to follow through in the future.  The key point is to “thrash” through the idea at the beginning and quickly decide if you’re going to execute or not.
