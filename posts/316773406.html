Look At Me

Shot entirely with the Canon 7D over three days, Patrick Lawler put together an interesting and beautiful film.  I love all of the close-up shots of the eyes using the Canon 100mm macro f/2.8 lens.  Hopefully Canon release the firmware update soon to allow for 60fps on the 5D Mark II as well.
