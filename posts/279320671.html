Carbon Dater

A beautifully designed black diamond-tipped carbon pen for designing and illustrating on glass.  I’m having a hard time deciding which I like more, the design of the pen or the inked 304-peice cardboard hinged packaging more.  At $1,000 for a set of ten I’ll simply have to enjoy the pictures of the pen.

Designed by Sruli Recht, glass illustrations by Jón Páll Halldórsson, and photography by Marino Thorlacius
