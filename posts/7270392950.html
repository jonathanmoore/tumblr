Atlantic Noir Theme

Over the weekend the latest Style Hatch theme for Tumblr, Atlantic Noir, went live!  For quite some time I have wanted to launch an extremely clean and modern theme that was designed with editorial content in mind.  With flexibility of the appearance options you can go from a colorful fashion feel on the demo site to dark and modern on the Empire—Atlantic Noir demo.

This is also our first theme to launch with true responsive design that adjusts based on your browser size or mobile device.  As you move to a smaller resolution the posts will get slightly less wide, switch to a single column and then go into a flexible layout for mobile devices.  Try it out on your iPad or iPhone.

Send us an email if you have any questions, feedback or suggestions - atlanticnoir@stylehatch.co

Also, make sure you check out the custom, non-Flash photoset player.
