The Web Designer’s Guide to Tumblr

Early this year .net magazine asked me to write a feature article for their April publication as an introduction and guide to Tumblr for web designers who aren’t familiar with Tumblr.  The 3,700 word article covers my personal experience with Tumblr, the community, going mobile, and themes.  If you’re already using Tumblr chances are nothing will be new to you, but if you do read it send me a reply on Twitter and let me know what you think - @newezra
