“Rusty Nails” by Moderate

Lately I have been drawn to creative executions that use simple and minimal elements to tell a story.  Design collective PFADFINDEREI based in Berlin, Germany used a combination of fabric, wind, well placed lights, dancers and illustrations for German music artist Moderate.

PFADFINDEREI’s other work is fantastic—their commissioned art installation for Nike, Yellow Lounge room design, film for Volvo DrivE, and various typefaces.
