Style Hatch Theme Updates

Earlier today major updates just went live for all six of the Style Hatch premium Tumblr themes.  Now all of the themes have Typekit support built in making it much easier to customize the fonts, as well as a number of other improvements and bug fixes.  Including the latest updates, this brings the total number of theme updates to 56 since the first theme launched nearly a year ago.  

The next round of updates will introduce image light boxes across the themes and support for the new Instagram API.

If you need help with your theme, have questions or even a suggestion I always do my best to reply in less than a day, with a bit slower responses over the weekend.  hello@stylehatch.co
