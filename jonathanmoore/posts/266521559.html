“Spacious Thoughts” N.A.S.A. featuring Tom Waits & Kool Keith

Montreal-based collective Fluorescent Hill created this colorful mixed media music video for N.A.S.A.  The scenes were created using photography of the Montreal shipyards which were then hand drawn and illustrated.  This music video might be my favorite motion piece of 2009.
