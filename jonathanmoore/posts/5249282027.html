night - by Mikko Lagerstedt

Compositing two images from the same location Mikko Lagerstedt created this moody view of the night sky.  Only two years ago Mikko picked up his first camera and taught himself photography.  Even more proof that “today everyone makes things”.  Imagine what it would have taken 30 years ago to get started in photography and show off your work to millions of people.
