Backburner 1.1 - New Share This Functionality

Backburner 1.1 was just submitted this morning for approval that is full of improvements, new features, and changes.  One new feature is the Share This tool below each post.  If you roll over the button you can copy the short URL, retweet using TweetMeme or share on Facebook.

In the upcoming Backburner 1.1.1 release I will be adding in support for Bit.ly which allows you to use their URL shortener and even roll your own shortener domain name with Bit.ly Pro.
