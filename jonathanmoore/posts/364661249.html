Myspace Fan Video

In an attempt to help revitalize the struggling Myspace brand, BBH London and interactive shop Domani Studios launched a site that adds your pictures into various popular music videos.  It certainly is not a new interactive gimmick, but it was executed very well.

One unexpected feature is it allows you to sign in with Facebook connect to import your photos.  Maybe it is a sign that they’re going to begin focusing on their strength in music.
