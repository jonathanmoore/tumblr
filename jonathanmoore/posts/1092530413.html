Nova Premium Theme

With nearly 50 appearance options, two layout modes—standard two column and HD-wide one column, black and white versions, and flexibility to change the theme colors as well as the background and header images, Nova is quite possibly the most customizable and flexible premium Tumblr theme.  Read the full description and features.

Additionally Nova is built on HTML5/CSS3 using a modified version of HTML5 Reset for the framework making it flexible for future updates and features.  As always Nova comes with a great deal of support—if you run into any issues, have suggestions or an idea share it on the theme feedback page or email me personally at nova@newezra.com.
