NBC’s fall line up bumpers dropped Klavika from last in favor of they humanist and nicely rounded Houschka Pro Extra Bold. I predict we’ll be seeing a lot of Houschka around this year as people discover it.

About Houschka
"Houschka has the humanist qualities reminiscent of Gill and Johnston but is quite unique. It is more monolinear with rounded corners giving it a softer appearance, and has a few interesting character quirks like the capitals A, G, Q, W and lower case u and w. Each weight contains alternate versions without the rounded A, W and w and with old-style numerals."
