I Make My Case

Case-Mate launches I Make My Case that allows you to create your own iPhone case using design elements from popular artists Matt W. Moore, Chuck Anderson, Joshua Davis, Nigel Dennis, Anthony Yankovic, Ray Frenden, and others.  Even if you’re not in the market to buy a new iPhone case checkout the site for it’s design and animation.

Site by Proto HQ, Edwin Tofslie, and Chuck Anderson.
